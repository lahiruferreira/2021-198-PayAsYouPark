Pay As You Park.

Automation Parking System in Sri Lanka

Introduction


Parking is a most critical task in smart cities, most of the time drivers have to ride far away from their destination to park their vehicles. With this situation drivers find it hard to park their vehicles. To avoid these kinds of situations people are used to parking their vehicle by breaking the rules and regulations. Due to that high traffic is now a common thing in urban areas. And most importantly wastage of fuel and emission of Carbon Dioxide is harmful to nature. Effects of these will be shown in near future. There are plenty of parking applications available but all of them have the same scenario and most users avoid them because they get charged unnecessarily. If smart cities got a proper smart parking solution, it will be the best solution for above mentioned issues. In this proposed system we are going to introduce ‘Pay as You Park’ Smart Parking Solution. With this system users are charged only for the time they have parked and the payment plan is based on a subscription package. End users receive the efficient service for their pay. And we will introduce many smart services for the users. Land owners in urban areas can join with us by registering their land or building with us, then after the analysing process we will add their land or building as a parking area to the system.

"Pay As You Park"


* Register Your Free Land or Space on our system

* Find the Shortest and Nearest way to Parking Area

* Internal Navigation Inside the Parking

* Pay for the time you Park your Vehicle

Research Project Scope

  • Literature Survey

    • People face different problems when they perform parking and the existing application has a huge gap towards what the end users are expecting. In Sri Lanka there is a remarkable wastage of fuel and air pollution in urban cities. With the presence of optimal parking suggestion in a smart parking solution it is aimed to solve the social issues present in the urban cities. When we consider about the parking yards exists there may be inappropriate parking yards which are not suitable for parking purpose. Users may face different problems and may get their vehicle damaged. There must be proper validation mechanism to approve a parking yard is suitable for parking or not. Also, tracking of free parking slots inside is a parking yard is an expensive process for the parking yard owners. Most parking solutions use sensor-based architecture for each slot. And yard owners have to invest a huge amount for those sensors. Minimizing the cost for the tracking if free slots inside a parking yard would be beneficial for the parking yard owners. Inside the big parking yards it is hard to identify the place to park easly so that proper Internal navigation parking system also should implemented as a solution to the current problems faced by people.




  • Research Problem

    • "Pay as you park" Smart parking solution coverd with diiferent aspect of users problem which they faced in day to day life in the urban cities. Mainly in the research work covers the problems such as identifying the empty parking lot in the parking yards, find the optimal way to manage ovaerall availability of parking place in productive way, Registering the new parking places in the system under suppervision of automated way of standardising method and lastly providing a Internal parking yard navigation inside of the parking areas. Providing the optimal solutions to those research problems our final product has designed to serve the people in Sri Lanka in smart way.

  • Research Objectives

    • The main objective of the full proposed system is to enhance and virtualize the system with smart features. The key features are make it easy for the user to manage their time and wastages while giving them a smart enovironment in parking industry.

    • A Convolutional Neural Network has been used to identify the availability of parking slots

    • Havesine algorithm used along with SARIMA model to provide optimal parking suggestions

    • To get accurate user positioning used beacon triangulation with Neural Network Algorithm

    • Identify the parking yard quality we used Convolutional Neural Network based trained model

Methodology

Identifying the quality of the new parking place and registering to the system

We have trained four model using the convolutional neural network (CNN) for the parking surface type classification and quality classification. Using the flask API we were able to mesure the quality before the new parking rgistration.

Find the availability of free space for parking inside a parking area

A Convolutional Neural Network has been used to identify the availability of parking slots. After processing the video frames of the CCTV footage it send the result of occupancy of parking slots to the server for distribution.

Suggest and direct to suitable parking areas based on vehicle model and features

Havesine algorithm used along with SARIMA model to provide optimal parking suggestions. Using fast API implementation we were able get real time parking suggestion values for the end user using mobile phone.

Internal Navigation in Parking Area

To get accurate user positioning used beacon triangulation with Neural Network Algorithm. Using the flask API, data will be inserted to mongo DB then use the Node express API to get the user position to the mobile app.
